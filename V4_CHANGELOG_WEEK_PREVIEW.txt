Driver Pay App V4 - Week Preview update

Changes:
- Reworked Week Preview modal.
- Header now shows Estimated Net, Payslip Net input, and colour-coded Difference.
- Days list now shows Hours, OT, KM, Bonuses text, and SR indicator.
- Days list does not show £ values.
- Added Show breakdown / Hide breakdown.
- Breakdown includes Base pay, Overtime, Bonuses, Food, Night out, Tax, NI, Net, and Split rests.
- End Week now opens a confirmation preview first.
- End Week confirmation shows Total hours, KM, Estimated Net, Payslip Net, Difference, Reduced rests, and Split rests.
- Confirm & Close Week remains available with options for remaining Off/Holiday days.

Unchanged:
- Day screen flow.
- v3 layout feel.
- Core calculations.
- Start/KM/rest logic.
