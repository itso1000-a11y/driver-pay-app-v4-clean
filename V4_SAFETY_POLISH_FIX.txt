V4 safety polish fix

Included fixes:
- End Week confirm is locked/debounced so double tapping cannot skip two weeks.
- Confirm & Close Week closes the preview immediately and opens the next week on Monday.
- Removed the long explanatory text from End Week preview to reduce visual clutter.
- Remaining Off / Remaining Holiday now affect only empty remaining days; entered work days are preserved.
- End Week now carries the last known km into Monday of the next week.
- Off/Holiday can no longer be applied to a day that already has entered work data; clear the day first.
- Off/Holiday days carry start km to finish km so km chain does not break.
